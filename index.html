Biography

Hu Cao is now a Ph.D. candidate in Chair of Robotics, AI and Real-time Systems, Technical University of Munich starting from Jan 2020. His research advisor is Prof. Alois Knoll. He graduated from State key laboratory of advanced design and manufacturing for vehicle body of Hunan University, China. Before coming to TUM, he did research in the Intelligent Automobile Research Institute of Automobile College of Tongji University. Hu Cao's research interests focus on Deep Representation Learning with Attention Mechanism for Dense Prediction (Classification, Detection, and Segmentation), Robotic Grasping, and Medical Image Analysis.

I am looking for highly self-motivated collaborators, who are interested in Autonomous Driving, Robotic Grasping and Medical Image Segmentation. Please send me up-to-date resume via email.
